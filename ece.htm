Vision

To nurture the students as a technocrat with new ideas and innovations in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering for the benefit of industry and society in the state and the country as a whole.


Mission
    To setup the department as a centre of excellence in the area of Electronics and Communication Engineering with focus on:
  • To develop the ability to understand the ethical values of research and development; and adaptability towards recent trends in technology.
  • To motivate and encourage the students for nucleation of new ideas and innovation by creating research environment for the benefit of the society.
  • To develop as a quality academic centre in order to produce students with professional expertise for empowering the industry with recent trends.
