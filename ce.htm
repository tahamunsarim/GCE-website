Head of Department: Dr. Nanjundaswamy Sateesh Kumar


Vision

Graduating civil engineers empowered with professional knowledge, skills and attitude contributing for sustainable development of the state and the nation.


Mission
    To promote quality technical education in the field of civil engineering through knowledge sharing,research and consultancy for satisfying the industrial and societal needs with a focus on:
  • Developing center of excellence in the field of civil engineering for promoting research in the emerging areas satisfying the global requirement.
  • Preparing the students in facing the competitive world and instill the thrust for indulging in life-long learning.
  • Strengthening the institute-industry interaction through information sharing and offering solutions to their problems.
  • Inculcating professional and technical knowledge imparting innovative thinking, ethical values and core competencies in nurturing the future civil engineers.
